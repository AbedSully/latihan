About Us

Founded in 2001, MediCare started as a small family-owned healthcare store in Kemanggisan, Jakarta. As more and more people get to know about MediCare, it is natural for us to expand our stores to other cities and provinces. Now, we have more than 100 stores spread out in almost every major cities in Indonesia, including but not limited to Aceh, Medan, Palembang, Jakarta, Surabaya, Pontianak, Balikpapan, dan Makassar. We also offer a wide variety of products from healthcare, cosmetics, medicines, and many more. We have a commitment to stay true to our humble origin and serving our customers with the best customer services and high quality products.
